Why Parametric Reduces Fraud

Because payouts are triggered by objective external data (rainfall mm, AQI index, GPS coordinates) — not self-reported claims — the attack surface for fraud is dramatically smaller. A fraudster cannot fake a city-wide AQI reading or a verified weather event.
